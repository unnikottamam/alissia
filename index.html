Petsitting Experiences

My petsitting experiences locally in Canada and abroad.

I began pet and caretaking in 2021 and have a total of 5+ years personal experiences handling all kinds of species in my care either in private or at someone's home with the help of a family member in my own country, alongside experiences which I did independently while abroad.

Poppy, Lily & Daisy

From April 2021, I started using the Canada Petsitting website where I started my petsitting journey. One of my first opportunities involved taking care of three wonderful Miniature Schnauzers locally.

New Zealand & Landlord Cat Care

In 2024, I did petsitting for a cat at a New Zealand address, and during that same year, I also cared for my landlord's pet, an indoor and outdoor long-haired grey cat.

Kauai, Hawaii (2025)

In 2025, I took care of three dogs—two French Bulldogs and a Labrador—on the island of Kauai in Hawaii, which I did on a long-term basis.

Caring for Animals Globally

The types of animals and experiences I have are mostly with smaller to larger sized breed dogs and cats, as well as farm animals. Over the course of the years, following and continuing doing what I love, I started looking at doing petsitting abroad.

I continued using Facebook groups as connection portals, which I use to combine my unique travel trips and fulfill owners' vacation requests.

My petsitting experience includes:

  • Caring and administering medications for pets requiring it.
  • Transporting pets that needed veterinarian care and attending already booked appointments.
  • Developing a physical routine and managing time, as well as being open and mindful of the physical activity and comfort level of the individual or group of pets.
  • Experience with anxiety and other related conditions, being aware of specific situations and surroundings.
